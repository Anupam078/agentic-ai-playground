COMPANY LEAVE POLICY

Casual Leave:
- Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves per year.
- Casual leave should be applied at least 1 day in advance whenever possible.

Sick Leave:
- Employees are entitled to 10 sick leaves per year.
- Medical certificate may be required for leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

Earned Leave:
- Employees earn 1.5 leave days per month.
- Unused earned leave can be carried forward to the next year.

General Rules:
- Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal.
- Unauthorized absence may result in disciplinary action.
